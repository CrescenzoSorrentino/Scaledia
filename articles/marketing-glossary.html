Practical Marketing Glossary

25 acronyms you really need to know

Do you know what ROAS is? CPL? CAC?
If they sound like Marvel robot names... you need this glossary.

In marketing, acronyms rain down like newsletter spam. The problem? Many use them to sound smart. Few truly understand them. You’ll be one of the few.

Below you’ll find 25 acronyms that actually matter. No jargon. Just clear definitions, real world examples, and zero fluff.

And if your favorite acronym is missing, message us: we’ll add it before it trends on TikTok.

A — The basics (that everyone pretends to know)

A/B Test – A test comparing two versions. Ex: two landing headlines, one gets double the conversions.

AIDA – Attention, Interest, Desire, Action. The classic sequence to hook and convert.

ARPU – Average Revenue Per User. Earn €10,000 from 500 users = ARPU €20.

B — Numbers that impress (or scare)

BR – Bounce Rate. Like guests who show up and leave a bad party.

B2B / B2C – Business to Business / Consumer. It changes everything: tone, channel, strategy.

C — Core digital metrics

CAC – Customer Acquisition Cost. Spend €100, get 5 clients = CAC €20.

CLV – Customer Lifetime Value. €30/month × 12 months = CLV €360.

CPC – Cost Per Click. €20 spent, 40 clicks = CPC €0.50.

CPM – Cost Per Mille (1,000 impressions). €50 for 10,000 views = CPM €5.

CPL – Cost Per Lead. €200 for 40 leads = CPL €5.

CTA – Call To Action. “Learn more”, “Download now”, “Buy today”.

CTR – Click Through Rate. 1,000 views, 50 clicks = CTR 5%.

K — Goals that actually matter (not vanity)

KPI – Key Performance Indicator. Conversions, ROAS, CLV... what really moves the needle.

L — Where the funnel begins (and often breaks)

Lead – An interested contact. Not a client yet, but could be.

Landing Page – A page built to convert. Every element pushes the user to act.

M — Subscriptions, revenue, and recurring metrics

MRR – Monthly Recurring Revenue. 50 subscribers × €20/month = MRR €1,000.

R — The numbers that really count

ROAS – Return On Ad Spend. Earn €500 with €100 spent = ROAS 5.

ROI – Return On Investment. €300 revenue - €200 cost = ROI 50%.

S — Free or Paid Visibility

SEO – Rank on Google for free. Write well = get found.

SEM – Paid ads on Google. You pay to be on top (Google Ads).

T — The Funnel Map

TOFU / MOFU / BOFU – Top, Middle, Bottom of Funnel. Each stage needs different content.

TAM – Total Addressable Market. Shows how big you can grow.

U — Stand Out or Fade Away

USP – Unique Selling Proposition. No USP = you're just a clone.
